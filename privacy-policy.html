02

Data We Collect

Data stored on your device (never transmitted to us):

  • Expenses, categories, and preferences you create within the app
  • App settings such as selected currency and theme

Important: All personal data you enter (such as expense records) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We have no access to this data.

Anonymous data collected automatically:

  • Anonymous usage events (e.g. which screens are visited, which features are used)
  • Crash reports and error logs to help us fix bugs
  • Device type and OS version (for compatibility purposes)

This anonymous data is collected via Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC). It is not linked to any personally identifiable information. We do not use the Advertising ID.

03

How We Use Data

  • To understand which features are most used and improve the app
  • To detect and fix crashes and technical issues
  • To make informed decisions about future features

We do not use any data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than improving the app experience.

04

Third-Party Services

Our apps use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) — anonymous usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) — crash reporting

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

05

Data Retention & Deletion

Data stored on your device is retained until you delete it within the app (via Settings → Delete All Data) or uninstall the app.

Anonymous analytics data is retained by Firebase according to Google's data retention policies (up to 14 months by default).

06

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through our apps, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.
