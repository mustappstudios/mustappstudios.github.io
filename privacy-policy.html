Privacy Policy –
Expense Tracker

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Expense Tracker is a personal finance app designed to help users keep track of their daily expenses.

User Data

Your expense records are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

Anonymous Usage Statistics

Anonymous usage data is collected via Google Firebase to improve the app experience. This data includes which screens are used and app crash reports. The collected data contains no personally identifiable information and is only associated with a randomly generated anonymous device identifier.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, provided by Google LLC. You can review their privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Advertising

This app displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use anonymous device identifiers for ad personalization. You can review Google's advertising privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Security

Your expense data is stored entirely on your device and is never sent to any server. The app allows you to export your data as a JSON file and save it to your device; this action only occurs at your explicit request.

Children's Privacy

This app is not designed for children under the age of 13.

Changes

Any updates to this privacy policy will be announced on this page.
